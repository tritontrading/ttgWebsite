Who We Are

About Us

Triton Trading Group (TTG) is a student-run nonprofit organization at UC San Diego focused on asset management, quantitative finance, and real-world business advisory work.

We provide students with hands-on experience in financial markets, portfolio management, and business strategy through structured training programs, real-world company engagements, and mentorship from industry professionals.

By combining education, practical experience, and collaboration, TTG helps students develop the analytical skills and professional mindset needed to succeed in finance, consulting, venture capital, and technology.

Our Mission

TTG is dedicated to advancing financial education, investment literacy, and professional development among students at UC San Diego.

Our mission is to provide members with practical exposure to capital markets, financial analysis, business strategy, and technological innovation through structured training programs and real-world experiences.

Through our departments in asset management, financial planning and analysis, and quantitative finance, TTG bridges the gap between academic learning and professional practice.

As a nonprofit, TTG reinvests its resources into educational programming, research initiatives, and opportunities that support the broader UCSD entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Asset Management

Student-managed investment portfolio with equity research, financial modeling, and structured investment committee presentations across six sector teams.

Financial Planning & Analysis

Real consulting-style projects helping companies and startups with financial analysis, market research, business strategy, and technology consulting.

Quantitative Finance

Data-driven investing and algorithmic trading strategies using Python, machine learning, statistical modeling, and professional platforms like QuantConnect.

Our Vision

We bridge classroom learning with real financial applications, preparing students for careers in asset management, hedge funds, investment banking, consulting, venture capital, fintech, and technology.

Our Mission

Members gain hands-on exposure to AI-powered tools, capital markets, and real-world advisory projects — developing the analytical skills and professional mindset needed to succeed across finance and technology sectors.

01Do you need any prior experience?▾
No prior experience is required. We welcome students from all majors and academic backgrounds. TTG provides structured training to develop analytical and financial skills from the ground up.
02What does Triton Trading Group offer?▾
TTG offers three departments: Asset Management (student-led investment portfolio), Financial Planning & Analysis (real company consulting engagements), and Quantitative Finance (algorithmic trading and ML models). Each provides hands-on experience, mentorship, and professional development.
03How can I join TTG?▾
TTG recruits each academic quarter. The process: (1) Submit an application, (2) Interview with TTG leadership, (3) Final selection and onboarding. New members start with introductory projects and core analytical training before specializing.
04What careers does TTG prepare students for?▾
Asset Management prepares students for hedge funds, asset management, and investment banking. FP&A prepares for consulting, corporate strategy, and VC. Quantitative Finance prepares for quant trading, fintech, and data science.
05Is TTG a nonprofit?▾
Yes. TTG is a student-run nonprofit that reinvests all resources into educational programming, research initiatives, and opportunities supporting student professional development and the broader UCSD entrepreneurial ecosystem.
