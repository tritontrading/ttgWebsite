Our Programs

TTG operates through three core departments. Each develops specialized skills while maintaining a strong foundation in analytical thinking, collaboration, and professional development.

Asset Management

Manage TTG's student-led investment portfolio. Conduct equity research, build financial models, write investment memos, and present investment ideas to the Investment Committee. Mirrors professional asset management firms with sector teams and portfolio oversight focused on risk management, diversification, and long-term value creation.
