Industry Partnerships

MarketCrunch AI

MarketCrunch AI is an AI-powered platform helping investors and traders analyze markets through advanced predictive analytics and financial data insights. Through this partnership, TTG members gain exposure to modern AI tools used in financial research and trading — directly informing the club's portfolio decisions and weekly analysis.

QuantConnect

QuantConnect is a professional algorithmic trading platform that allows users to design, test, and deploy systematic trading strategies. TTG's Quantitative Finance department uses QuantConnect to develop algorithmic models and perform backtesting using real market data — providing professional-grade technical experience.
