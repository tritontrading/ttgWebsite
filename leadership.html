Board Member Responsibilities

Board Members support the Executive Board and help lead teams within TTG departments — leading sector research teams in Asset Management, managing FP&A project engagements, supporting quantitative research initiatives, and mentoring general members.

General Members by Class

Class of 2026

Senior members participating in leadership roles and advanced projects — transitioning into advisory roles upon graduation to continue mentoring future members.

Class of 2027

Members developing analytical and technical skills within departments, contributing to equity research, consulting projects, and quantitative strategies.

Class of 2028

New members beginning their TTG experience and participating in introductory projects across Asset Management, FP&A, and Quantitative Finance.

Advisors

TTG is supported by advisors and industry professionals who provide mentorship and strategic guidance. Advisors assist by providing insight into financial markets and business strategy, mentoring students pursuing careers in finance and consulting, and supporting the development of TTG programs and partnerships.
