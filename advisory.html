Measurable Impact

Track performance, uncover insights, and turn data into actionable proof points. Every engagement produces outputs you can use immediately and maintain after handoff.

We exist to help early-stage teams make high-stakes decisions with clarity — turning uncertain questions into structured choices that founders can defend with investors and clients with their current resources.

We deliver this through a student-run model designed for leverage: tight scope, fast iteration, and rigorous internal review. Every engagement is built around transparent assumptions, clean methodology, and practical outputs that clients can use immediately and maintain after handoff.

We bring this purpose to life across the decisions that matter most in early-stage growth: runway planning, unit economics, pricing and packaging, go-to-market tradeoffs, and capital readiness. By staying focused on one client at a time, we protect accountability and produce measurable outcomes — while training our members on real constraints, real standards, and real responsibility.
